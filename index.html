About Adventure Banyuwangi

Discover the Hidden Paradise of East Java

Hi! We’re passionate travelers and proud locals of Banyuwangi – the sunrise gateway of Java, Indonesia. Through this website, we want to share with the world the incredible beauty and adventures that Banyuwangi offers.

From the mystical blue fire of Ijen Crater, the untamed waves of G-Land for surfers, to the lush rainforests of Alas Purwo, Banyuwangi is a treasure trove waiting to be explored.

Unlike Bali’s crowds, Banyuwangi offers a more authentic, untouched experience – where nature and culture still live in harmony. Whether you love hiking mountains, chasing waterfalls, or diving into local traditions, Banyuwangi has something just for you.

This site was created to guide travelers—especially international adventurers—who seek new horizons off the beaten path. We hope you feel the magic of our land just like we do.
