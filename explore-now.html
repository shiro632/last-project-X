Pulau Merah

Famous for its small red island and sunset views, Pulau Merah Beach offers great waves for beginner surfers and a scenic coastline..

Teluk Hijau

Tucked inside Meru Betiri National Park, Teluk Hijau offers emerald-green waters, white sand, and a peaceful tropical forest surrounding.

Bama

Located in Baluran National Park, Bama Beach is calm and shallow, perfect for snorkeling and spotting wildlife like monkeys and deer..

Cacalan

Cacalan Beach features calm waves and a peaceful atmosphere. It’s great for family picnics and enjoying traditional fishing boats..

Bangsring

Bangsring Beach is known for its floating house and coral conservation. It’s a top spot for snorkeling and marine education..

Watu Dodol

Located by the roadside, Watudodol Beach offers clear waters and a unique underwater statue. It’s a great stopover for snorkeling.

Rajeg Wesi

A quiet beach used by local fishermen, Rajegwesi is also the gateway to Green Bay. The surrounding cliffs add natural charm.

Cemara

Named after the pine trees (cemara), this beach offers a shady escape and calm waves—ideal for relaxing or cycling along the shore..

Pancur

Found inside Meru Betiri National Park, Pancur Beach is quiet and natural. It’s often the resting point before trekking to Sukamade.

Batu

A less-known beach with rocky formations, Batu Beach is perfect for those seeking quiet and natural scenery off the beaten path.

Bedul

Located in Alas Purwo National Park, Bedul Beach features mangrove ecosystems and a boat ride that connects to Segara Anakan lagoon.

Mustika

Next to Pulau Merah, Mustika Beach is peaceful and less crowded. It’s great for watching local fishing activities in the morning.

Sabo

Sobo Beach is located close to Banyuwangi city and is known for its calm waters and peaceful vibe. It's great for a quiet sunset walk and enjoying the view of fishing boats.

Lampon

A small, secluded beach near Pesanggaran, Lampon offers a quiet retreat with rocky cliffs and golden sand.

Sukamade

One of Banyuwangi’s treasures, Sukamade is known for sea turtle conservation. Visitors can witness turtle nesting at night.

Wedi Ireng

Hidden and adventurous, Wedi Ireng offers a mix of black and white sand, perfect for explorers seeking untouched beauty.

Pulau Santen

Pulau Santen, or Santen Island, offers a unique beach experience with a view of Mount Ijen in the background. Recently revitalized, it’s known for its clean coastline and chill atmosphere, perfect for relaxing or photo spots.

Gunung

Gunung Ijen

Ijen Crater is famous for its mesmerizing turquoise acid lake and the rare blue fire phenomenon. It's also home to traditional sulfur miners who work under extreme conditions..

Gunung Raung

Mount Raung is one of the most active volcanoes in East Java, known for its wide caldera and challenging trekking route. It's a favorite for adventurous climbers seeking a thrilling hike..

Kawah Wurung

Kawah Wurung offers vast green savannas and a stunning volcanic landscape. Unlike typical craters, it's known for its peaceful atmosphere and panoramic views.

Gunung Ranti

Mount Ranti is a relatively easy hike and often serves as an alternative route to Ijen Crater. It offers beautiful sunrise views overlooking Ijen and the surrounding forests.

Bukit Mondoleko

Bukit Mondoleko is a scenic hill known for its peaceful environment and wide view of rice fields and nearby mountains. It’s ideal for camping and sunrise watching.

Gunung Gumitir

Mount Gumitir is located between Banyuwangi and Jember, surrounded by tea and coffee plantations. It's a historical area with cool air and misty landscapes, perfect for a relaxing escape.
